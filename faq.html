Got Questions? We Have Answers!

Before asking us any questions via the contact page check out bellow if we have already answered your questions. If you do not happen to see your question or the answer listed is unsatisfactory then feel free to message us using The Contact Page

How fast are your delivery times?

We try to ensure at least a week for delivery. Of course time may vary based on concentrate availability and size of shipments. If you have further questions about product availability feel free to reach out via phone call or email and we will try to get to your questions within a day.

What alergins do you cater to?

We strive to ensure that our juice concentrates do not trigger alergins. We make sure to wash and process all produce and keep it separated to be certian you know that your children will enjoy our lovely selections of juices!

Are your juices fresh squeezed?

We provide 100% blended fruit juice concentrates that are specially made for kids and meet all the requirements of the FDA with regard to subsidy and nutrition. For more info check our ingredients tab.

How much juice can be made per case?

One case contains 6 half gallon containers of concentrate which in total produces 24 gallons of juice once mixed properly. You can order up to 6 flavors per case. You must order at least one case of 6.

Do I have to work for a daycare to order?

It is our policy that we only sell to daycare facilities as our juice is ment only for locations and events where the juice will be consumed in mass. We reseve the right to deny service to those not assocuated with a daycare facility.
